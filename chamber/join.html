Join the Willingboro Chamber of Commerce

Non-Profit Membership - Free

  • Available for registered non-profit organizations. Enjoy Silver-level benefits at no cost!

Bronze Membership - $250/year

  • Business listing in the Chamber’s online directory
  • Access to networking events & mixers (discounted entry)
  • Member-only discounts on select Chamber events
  • Subscription to the Chamber’s newsletter
  • Eligibility to join industry-specific committees
  • Use of Chamber logo on website & marketing materials

Silver Membership - $500/year

  • All Bronze Membership benefits
  • Discounted advertising opportunities in Chamber publications
  • One free admission to select training workshops or webinars per year
  • Special recognition at annual Chamber events

Gold Membership - $1,000/year

  • All Silver Membership benefits
  • Premium listing & rotating spotlight feature on Chamber homepage
  • Monthly social media spotlight post
  • Free admission to all Chamber training workshops/webinars
  • Exclusive VIP networking events & early registration access
  • Priority sponsorship opportunities with promotional branding
  • Recognition as a Chamber advocate in email campaigns
