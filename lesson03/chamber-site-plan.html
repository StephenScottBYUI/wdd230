Willingboro Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Willingboro Chamber of Commerce

The name gets straight to the point about the purpose of the website, a homepage for the Willingboro CoC.

Site Purpose

  1. Promoting Local Businesses:
    • The website will serve as a platform to showcase and support local businesses in Willingboro, NJ. Leading to economic growth within the community.
  2. Community Egagement and Information:
    • This website will act as a bridge between businesses and community members. This is where community members can find information about events, resources, and opportunities.
  3. Grow CoC Membership:
    • It will be a place to promote the benefits of joining the CoC. It will also provide a place for local business owners to easily join the CoC.

Scenarios

  1. What events and workshops are available for local business networking this month?
  2. How do I become a member of the Willingboro Chamber of Commerce?
  3. Where can I find resources for starting or expanding a business in Willingboro?
  4. Who are the current board members, and how can I contact them?
  5. What are the key industries thriving in Willingboro?

Colors

Background Color

#F2E4BB will be the background color and the text color when the background is dark

Heading Color

#59163B will be the color for headers and footers

Text and Border Color

#060126 is the color that will be used for text on light backgrounds

Navigation Color

#79361E will be the background color for the navigation menus

Call to Action Button

#88C057 will be the button color for call to actions and the highlight color for when the mouse hovers over a menu option

Typography

Montserrat

This sans-serif font will be for headins and subheadings

Roboto

This will be the main text font used throughout the website

Playfair Display

This will be used sparingly as an accent font for quotes or featured text
